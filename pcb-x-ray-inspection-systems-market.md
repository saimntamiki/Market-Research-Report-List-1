<p><p>PCB X-Ray Inspection Systems are essential for evaluating the quality of printed circuit boards. There are two main types: 2D X-Ray Inspection Systems provide flat images, allowing for quick inspection of surface features and internal structures. They are cost-effective for initial quality checks. In contrast, 3D X-Ray Inspection Systems offer volumetric imaging, enabling detailed analysis of complex geometries and hidden defects. This advanced technology enhances defect detection and assures higher-quality manufacturing, catering to industries requiring precision and reliability.</p></p>
