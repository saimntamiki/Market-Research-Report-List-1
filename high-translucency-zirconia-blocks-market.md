<p><ul><li>Multilayer Zirconia Blocks</li><li>Monolithic Zirconia Blocks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High translucency zirconia blocks are primarily categorized into two types: multilayer zirconia blocks and monolithic zirconia blocks. Multilayer zirconia blocks feature varying levels of translucency and shade throughout their thickness, allowing for aesthetic results that mimic natural teeth, making them ideal for anterior restorations. In contrast, monolithic zirconia blocks are single-layered and provide enhanced strength and durability, suitable for posterior restorations where resilience is crucial. Both types are increasingly popular in dental applications due to their superior aesthetics and performance.</p></p>
