<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Molded Aspheric Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Biconvex</li><li>Convex-concave</li><li>Biconcave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Molded aspheric lenses are specialized optical components designed to reduce spherical aberrations and enhance image quality. The market includes various types: </p><p>1. **Biconvex lenses** have two outward-curved surfaces, ideal for converging light and used in imaging applications. </p><p>2. **Convex-concave lenses** feature one outward-curved and one inward-curved surface, balancing light divergence and convergence for versatile applications. </p><p>3. **Biconcave lenses** have two inward-curved surfaces, primarily used for diverging light, often in laser systems and optical instruments. Each type serves specific optical requirements across industries.</p></p>
