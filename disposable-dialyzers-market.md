<p><strong>The Disposable Dialyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Flux Membrane</li><li>High Flux Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disposable dialyzers market is categorized into low flux and high flux membrane types. Low flux dialyzers utilize membranes that allow small molecules to pass through, making them suitable for patients with stable renal function, requiring less clearance. In contrast, high flux dialyzers feature membranes designed to filter larger molecules and facilitate better removal of toxins, catering to patients with more acute renal issues. This distinction impacts treatment efficacy, patient outcomes, and overall market demand for specific dialyzer types.</p></p>
