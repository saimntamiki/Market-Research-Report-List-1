<p><p>Shrink film packaging is categorized into two main types: printed and unprinted shrink film. Printed shrink film features custom designs, logos, and graphics that enhance product branding, making items visually appealing on shelves. This type is often used for promotional purposes. In contrast, unprinted shrink film is clear or transparent, providing a protective cover without branding elements. It is commonly utilized for bundling products or providing tamper evidence while maintaining visibility, making it suitable for a variety of applications.</p></p>
