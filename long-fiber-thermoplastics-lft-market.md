<p><ul><li>LFT-G</li><li>LFT-D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Long Fiber Thermoplastics (LFT) are composite materials that incorporate continuous fiber reinforcement within a thermoplastic matrix, enhancing mechanical properties and durability. The LFT-G (Long Fiber Thermoplastic Granulates) market focuses on granulated forms that allow easy processing and are often used in injection molding applications. In contrast, the LFT-D (Long Fiber Thermoplastic Direct) market emphasizes direct fiber incorporation during processing, which improves fiber alignment and performance in final products. Both markets cater to industries like automotive and aerospace, seeking lightweight, robust materials.</p></p>
