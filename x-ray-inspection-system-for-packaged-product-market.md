<p><p>The X-Ray Inspection System for Packaged Products market primarily includes two types: Digital Radiography (DR) and Computed Tomography (CT). DR employs digital detectors to capture images in real-time, enabling quick evaluations of product integrity and safety, making it ideal for high-throughput environments. In contrast, CT provides detailed, cross-sectional images by combining multiple X-ray data, allowing for more comprehensive inspections of product packaging, including density variations. Both technologies enhance quality control and facilitate compliance in various industries.</p></p>
