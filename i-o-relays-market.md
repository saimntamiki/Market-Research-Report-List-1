<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Relay</li><li>Solid State Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The I & O relays market comprises two main types: electromagnetic relays (EMRs) and solid-state relays (SSRs). EMRs utilize mechanical components to open or close circuits through electromagnetic induction, making them suitable for high-power applications. In contrast, SSRs employ semiconductor devices to switch circuits without moving parts, offering faster response times, greater durability, and resistance to shock and vibration. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automation, transportation, and telecommunications, ensuring efficient signal management and system control.</p></p>
