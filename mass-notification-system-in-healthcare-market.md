<p><p>Mass Notification Systems (MNS) in healthcare are essential for ensuring rapid communication during emergencies. In-building Mass Notification Systems are designed for hospitals and clinics, delivering alerts through intercoms and alarms within a facility. Outdoor Mass Notification Systems target external areas, using sirens or loudspeakers to inform the public during crises.
