<p><p>Cone Beam Imaging Systems (CBIS) are categorized into standing and seated market types based on the positioning of patients during imaging. Standing systems allow patients to maintain an upright posture, which is beneficial for capturing accurate anatomical details and is often used in orthopedic and dental applications. Seated systems, on the other hand, offer comfort for patients who may struggle to stand for extended periods, making them suitable for various clinical environments, including oral and maxillofacial imaging. Both types enhance diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
