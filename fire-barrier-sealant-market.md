<p><strong>The Fire Barrier Sealant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Elastometric Type</li><li>Intumescent Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire barrier sealant market consists of two primary types: elastomeric and intumescent sealants. Elastomeric sealants are flexible materials that provide effective sealing against fire, smoke, and gases while allowing for building movements. Intumescent sealants, on the other hand, expand when exposed to high temperatures, forming a thick barrier that protects against flames and smoke. Both types are essential for ensuring fire safety and compliance in various construction applications, contributing to overall building integrity.</p></p>
