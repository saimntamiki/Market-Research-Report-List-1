<p><ul><li>Density Gradient Centrifugation</li><li>Chemical Lysis</li><li>Mechanical Lysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Nuclei Isolation Kit market comprises three main types: Density Gradient Centrifugation, Chemical Lysis, and Mechanical Lysis. Density Gradient Centrifugation separates nuclei based on density differences, ensuring high purity and viability. Chemical Lysis utilizes specific reagents to break down cell membranes, allowing for efficient nuclei extraction while preserving their integrity. Mechanical Lysis employs physical methods, such as homogenization or shearing forces, to disrupt cells and isolate nuclei. Each method caters to different research needs and sample types in biological studies.</p></p>
