<p><p>The Integral Waterproofing Compound market consists of two main types: liquid-based and powder-based products. Liquid-based compounds are formulated to be mixed directly with concrete or mortar, enhancing fluidity and providing seamless waterproofing. They are generally easier to apply and offer immediate integration. In contrast, powder-based compounds are mixed with water or other ingredients before application, often providing a more robust solution suitable for various construction needs. Both types serve to effectively prevent water ingress, enhancing the longevity of structures.</p></p>
