<p><strong>The Orris Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steam Distillation</li><li>Leaching Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Orris oil is extracted primarily through two methods: steam distillation and the leaching method. </p><p>In steam distillation, the dried rhizomes of the iris plant are subjected to steam, which helps separate the volatile oils, producing a concentrated fragrance used in perfumes and cosmetics. </p><p>The leaching method involves soaking the dried rhizomes in a solvent, which extracts the oil, after which the solvent is removed, leaving behind a fragrant oil. Both methods cater to varying market demands for high-quality orris oil in various industries.</p></p>
