<p><ul><li>Intumescent Fire Retardant Cable Coatings</li><li>Non-Intumescent Fire Retardant Cable Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fire Retardant Cable Coatings market is primarily categorized into two types: intumescent and non-intumescent coatings. Intumescent fire retardant coatings expand when exposed to heat, creating a protective char layer that insulates cables from flames. In contrast, non-intumescent coatings do not expand but instead form a barrier that inhibits the ignition and spread of fire. Both types are essential for enhancing electrical cable safety in various environments, ensuring compliance with fire safety regulations and protecting infrastructure.</p></p>
