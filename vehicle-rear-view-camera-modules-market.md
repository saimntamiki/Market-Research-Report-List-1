<p><p>Vehicle rear view camera modules primarily include CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras. CCD cameras are known for their superior image quality, low noise levels, and better performance in low light, making them ideal for high-end vehicles. In contrast, CMOS cameras offer smaller size, lower power consumption, and faster processing speeds, making them popular in mainstream models. Both technologies enhance safety and convenience by providing clear visual guidance when reversing or parking vehicles.</p></p>
