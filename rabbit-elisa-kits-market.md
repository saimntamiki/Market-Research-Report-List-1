<p><p>Rabbit ELISA kits are categorized based on the type of detection system used. Unconjugated kits rely on primary antibodies for direct binding, while alkaline phosphatase kits utilize enzyme conjugates to amplify signal detection. Biotin-based kits employ biotin-labeled antibodies and avidin interaction for enhanced sensitivity. Other types may include variations such as horseradish peroxidase or fluorescence-based systems. Each type offers specific advantages, catering to diverse laboratory needs and improving the versatility and accuracy of immunoassays.</p></p>
