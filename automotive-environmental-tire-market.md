<p><p>The automotive environmental tire market is primarily segmented into two types: all-steel and semi-steel tires. All-steel tires are constructed with a steel belt and casing, offering enhanced durability, performance, and resistance to wear, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, semi-steel tires combine steel belts with polyester or nylon, resulting in a lighter, more flexible tire that still provides good strength and performance. Both types are designed to meet eco-friendly standards, minimizing environmental impact while maximizing efficiency.</p></p>
