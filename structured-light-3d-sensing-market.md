Coded Structured Light employs unique patterns to enhance spatial resolution and accuracy, making it suitable for intricate surface analysis. Speckle Structured Light utilizes random light patterns, enabling robust depth sensing in challenging environments, particularly in industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
