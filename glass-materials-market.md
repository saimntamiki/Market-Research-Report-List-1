<p><p>The glass materials market includes various types: </p><p>1. **Aluminosilicate** – Known for high thermal stability and resistance to temperature changes, suitable for applications like cookware and laboratory equipment. </p><p>2. **Borosilicate** – Offers excellent thermal resistance and chemical durability, commonly used in laboratory glassware and kitchen items. </p><p>3. **Carbon Glassy** – Combines carbon and glass features, used in specialized applications where lightweight and strength are essential. </p><p>4. **Glass Ceramics** – Formed through controlled crystallization, exhibiting high strength, thermal stability, and is utilized in dental and cookware applications.</p><p>5. **Quartz** – A naturally occurring mineral, famous for its optical clarity and thermal resistance, widely used in electronics and optics.</p></p>
