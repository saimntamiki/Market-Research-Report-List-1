<p><p>The Data Centre Power Distribution Unit (PDU) market is categorized into several types. Metered PDUs allow for tracking of power usage at a basic level, helping optimize consumption. Switched PDUs provide greater control by allowing individual outlet management and remote operations, enabling better energy management and reducing downtime risk in data centre operations.</p></p>
