<p><p>Bilingual school education encompasses various market types: </p><p>1. **Immersion Model**: Students primarily learn in a second language, enhancing fluency while acquiring core subjects, fostering bilingualism from an early age.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Transitional Bilingual Model**: Initially uses the native language to teach, gradually shifting to the target language, helping students transition to English proficiency.</p><p>  </p><p>3. **Maintenance Model**: Focuses on preserving native language skills while developing proficiency in a second language, promoting biliteracy and cultural identity. Each model addresses different educational needs and goals.</p></p>
