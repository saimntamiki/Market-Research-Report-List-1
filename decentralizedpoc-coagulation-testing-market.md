Whole blood specimen testing kits enable immediate results by analyzing untreated blood samples at the site of care, enhancing rapid decision-making. Meanwhile, satellite anticoagulation therapy encompasses remote monitoring and management solutions, allowing healthcare providers to ensure effective anticoagulation therapy while minimizing patient visits to clinical settings.</p></p>
