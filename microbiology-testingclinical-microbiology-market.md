<p><p>The microbiology testing market encompasses various methods for identifying and analyzing microorganisms. Cell culture involves growing organisms in controlled environments to study their characteristics and behavior. Microscopy employs optical instruments to visualize and differentiate microorganisms based on their morphology. Serology analyzes blood serum for antibodies or antigens to detect infections or immune responses. Each method plays a crucial role in clinical microbiology, aiding diagnosis, treatment decisions, and monitoring of infectious diseases while ensuring public health safety.</p></p>
