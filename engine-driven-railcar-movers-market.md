<p><ul><li>Single Coupled</li><li>Double Coupled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Engine-driven railcar movers are specialized vehicles used to reposition railcars within industrial yards or rail terminals. The single coupled type features a single coupling mechanism, allowing for simplified maneuverability and operation with individual railcars. In contrast, double coupled railcar movers utilize two coupling mechanisms, providing enhanced stability and the ability to handle multiple railcars simultaneously. Each type serves distinct operational needs, with single coupled movers typically favored for versatility in tight spaces and double coupled models preferred for heavier loads and complex movements.</p></p>
