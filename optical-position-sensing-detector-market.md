<p><p>The Optical Position Sensing Detector market encompasses various types, including Dual Cell and Quadrant detectors, among others. Dual Cell sensors utilize two photodetector elements to measure positional information based on light intensity differences, ideal for linear applications. Quadrant detectors, divided into four segments, enhance angular and spatial resolution, suitable for precise alignment tasks. Other types may include more specialized sensors catering to specific applications, such as laser machining or aerospace, facilitating high-accuracy positioning and tracking capabilities across diverse industries.</p></p>
