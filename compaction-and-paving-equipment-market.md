<p><ul><li>Compaction Rollers</li><li>Jumping Jacks/Rammers</li><li>Pavers</li><li>Plate Compactors</li><li>Scrapers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The compaction and paving equipment market includes several key types that cater to various construction needs. Compaction rollers are used for large-scale surface compaction, ensuring a solid foundation. Jumping jacks or rammers compact soil in confined spaces, ideal for trenches. Pavers are employed for laying asphalt or concrete, creating smooth surfaces. Plate compactors deliver high-frequency vibration for compacting granular materials. Scrapers are utilized for earthmoving tasks, efficiently leveling and transporting soil and materials across sites.</p></p>
