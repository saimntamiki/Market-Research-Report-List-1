<p><p>The bleached absorbent cotton market comprises two primary types: virgin cotton and noil. Virgin cotton is derived from high-quality, untreated cotton fibers, ensuring maximum absorbency and purity, making it ideal for medical and personal care applications. In contrast, noil is the shorter, coarser fibers left after the processing of virgin cotton, often used in industrial applications or as a cost-effective alternative. Both types cater to diverse needs, balancing quality and affordability within the absorbent cotton sector.</p></p>
