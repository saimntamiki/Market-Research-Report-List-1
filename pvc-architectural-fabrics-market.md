<p><p>The PVC architectural fabrics market primarily includes two types: polyester fiber fabric and polyamide fiber fabric. Polyester fiber fabric is known for its durability, weather resistance, and UV stability, making it ideal for outdoor structures like awnings and canopies. Polyamide fiber fabric, while strong and flexible, excels in applications requiring high tensile strength and stretchability, such as tents and inflatable structures. Both types offer versatility in design, enabling innovative architectural solutions while ensuring longevity and performance.</p></p>
