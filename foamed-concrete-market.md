<p><strong>The Foamed Concrete Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Density</li><li>Medium Density</li><li>High Density</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The foamed concrete market is categorized into three main types based on density: low density, medium density, and high density. Low-density foamed concrete, typically ranging from 300 to 1,000 kg/m³, is lightweight and offers excellent thermal insulation, making it suitable for lightweight fill and insulation applications. Medium-density foamed concrete, between 1,000 and 1,800 kg/m³, provides a balance of strength and insulation, often used in residential and commercial construction. High-density foamed concrete, exceeding 1,800 kg/m³, offers superior strength and durability, ideal for structural applications and heavy-load scenarios.</p></p>
