<p><ul><li>Ionisation Type</li><li>Photoelectric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The domestic smoke alarms market primarily consists of two types: ionization and photoelectric alarms. Ionization smoke alarms are sensitive to fast-burning fires, using radioactive materials to detect smoke particles. Photoelectric alarms, on the other hand, excel in detecting smoldering fires, utilizing a light source and sensor to identify smoke. Both types play crucial roles in home safety, with consumers often choosing based on fire risk assessment, preferences for technology, and cost considerations, leading to a diverse market landscape.</p></p>
