<p><strong>The Pediatric Hip Orthoses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed</li><li>Hip Articulated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pediatric hip orthoses market comprises two primary types: fixed and hip articulated orthoses. Fixed hip orthoses immobilize the hip joint to stabilize it, often used in conditions like developmental dysplasia of the hip. In contrast, hip articulated orthoses allow limited movement, promoting controlled mobility while providing support. These devices cater to various orthopedic needs in children, including post-surgical recovery and treatment of muscular or skeletal disorders, enhancing mobility and ensuring proper hip alignment during growth.</p></p>
